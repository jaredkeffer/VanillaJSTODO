Gradient Editor

background:

Pressing + creates a new list, with a maxiumum of 10 per page.

When on the last item of the list, pressing tab creates a new field.

Pressing - on the field deletes that field. Pressing - on the title of the list deletes the entire list.

If there are too many items in the list to display, you can scroll within the list to see all items.

Pasting a color or linear gradient into the field under 'background: ' in the menu and pressing enter changes the background.

Clicking 'Gradient Editor' in the menu takes you to one of my favorite gradient editors.

You can create as many pages as you like! Pressing the save button or changing the background saves all data to your machine.
